About Me

Hi my name is Derek Santiago. I am a test engineer for Brayton Energy in Hampton New Hampshire. I recieved my batchelors degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of New Hampshire in May 2023. I also have two assocciates degrees form New Hampshire Technical Institute in Mechanical Engineering and Manufacturing Engineering.

I have been at Brayton Energy for 3 and half years and started as a mechanical technition operating heavy equipment, assembling heat exchangers and recuperators, welding and fabricating project test equipment, and assembling testing high pressure gas lines. Since recieving my batchelors degree I have transitioned out of the technition role and more into an engineer role woking on designing componets in SolidWorks, running tests with National Instuments LabView, seting up required DAQ equipent for testing, and working with high and low voltage lines for operating test equiment.

I am an Army veteran who was stationed in Fort Drum New York for 4 years and currently still serve in the Army Reserves. Combined I have 12 years in service with one deployment to Syria. My MOS is a 91B also know as diesel technition. While in the reserves I have been certified in crane lifing opperations and vehical recovery opperations.>
